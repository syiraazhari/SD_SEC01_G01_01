About Us

The current economic system has failed many Malaysians in terms of wages, jobs, wealth creation, and opportunity. This failure is manifested through rising inequality and the widening gap between the haves and have-nots, especially among the Bumiputera demographic, which is concentrated in rural areas.The number of hardcore poor in Kuala Lumpur shot up by nearly 270% over two years. Data from the Federal Territories Ministry showed that there were 3,865 hardcore poor families in 2021 compared to only 1,048 in 2019.The number of poor families in the same period also showed a drastic 41% increase, going from 2,871 in 2019 to 4,051 last year.

Many people want to give donation but are still hesitant because there is no reliable website. So, we create a website to help people in need such as the homeless, lack of daily income and also open job opportunities to cover the cost of daily life.
